Mekuri Realm

A collectible card game with fantasy art from the "Edge Series: Realm", grid-based tactical gameplay, and a risk-based progression system where every card matters.

in development
~/about

Mekuri Realm is a mobile card game inspired by trading card games, video game classics, and a love for fantasy worlds. Collect cards, build strategic decks, and battle opponents on a grid-based battlefield. Every match has real stakes — win to claim your opponent's cards, but lose and one of yours is gone.

Card art comes from the "Edge Series: Realm" collection — artwork featuring creatures, artifacts, and landscapes across six unique biomes.

~/features
Grid-Based Battles
Place cards on a 4×4 grid. Capture adjacent cards through directional combat with combo chains that can flip the entire board.
69 Collectible Cards
46 creatures, 14 artifacts, 6 evolution exclusives, and 3 mutations across six fantasy biomes. Normal and foil variants.
Damage Types
Physical, Elemental, Magical — a rock-paper-scissors triangle that adds +1 to combat. Build diverse decks to gain the edge.
Risk & Reward
Win cards from opponents or lose yours. Starter cards are protected, but everything else is at stake. No save-scumming.
Card Experience
Cards level up through victories (Rank 0–4). Each rank boosts a random side by +1. Lose all copies and the experience is gone.
Card Evolution
Three fusion pathways — creature chains, free mutation, and artifact evolution. Discover exclusive cards through the fusion system.
6 AI Ranks
From novice to grandmaster. The AI adapts with deeper lookahead, trap-setting, and type-aware strategies. Staggered difficulty ensures fair progression.
Six Biomes
Plains, Swamp, Forest, Island, Mountains, and the Void — each with unique grid theming, ambient audio, and opponent characters.
